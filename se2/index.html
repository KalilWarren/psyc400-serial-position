Self-Experiment 2: Word Memory

In this task you will see a series of words. For each word you will answer a short question about it.

  • Some words appear with the question "How many vowels are in this word?" Type the number and press Enter.
  • Other words appear inside a sentence with a blank, with the question "Does this word fit in the sentence?" Choose Yes or No.
  • Work as quickly and accurately as you can. Answer each question based on your first judgement.

All participants begin with a short practice round using different words. The practice does not count toward your results.
