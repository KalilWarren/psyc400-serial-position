Self-Experiment 1: The Serial Position Effect

This task measures memory for lists of words. Please read the instructions before you begin.

  • Words will appear on the screen one at a time. Study each word as it appears.
  • Do not write anything down while the words are being presented.
  • After each list, you will have 90 seconds to type as many words as you can recall. Order does not matter.
  • The experiment consists of three lists presented in sequence.
  • It begins with one short practice trial, followed by the three experimental trials.

All participants complete the same short practice trial before the experiment begins. The practice uses a different set of words, so it does not affect your results.
