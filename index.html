Self-Experiment 1: The Serial Position Effect

Welcome. This activity tests how well you remember a list of words.

  • Words will appear on the screen one at a time. Study each word as it appears.
  • Do not write anything down while the words are showing.
  • After each list, you will have 90 seconds to type every word you can remember. Order does not matter.
  • You will do this three times with three different lists.
  • First you'll do one quick practice trial to get used to how it works, then the real experiment begins.

Everyone starts with a short practice round so the warm-up is the same for all students. The practice uses different words than the real experiment, so it won't affect your results.

When you are ready, enter your name and begin the practice.
